\begin{abstract}
We report the discovery of a discrete geometric structure underlying the Standard Model particle mass spectrum. By expressing particle masses as logarithmic indices relative to the electron mass, we find that all known particles occupy integer lattice sites governed by the golden ratio. The lattice admits a natural embedding into the representation theory of the alternating group $A_5$, revealing a previously unrecognized geometric organization of particle masses. The construction is parameter-free, empirically stable, and yields sharp, falsifiable predictions for new particles corresponding to unoccupied lattice sites.
\end{abstract}
